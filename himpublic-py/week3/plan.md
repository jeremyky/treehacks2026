# Week 3: Content & Iteration

**Goal:** Film content, post to socials, see what works

---

## Day 1-2: Content Filming

### Shot List

#### 1. Adam Watches Sports
- Set up TV/monitor with sports highlights
- Adam reacts in real-time to goals, misses
- Film from angle showing both screen and Adam

#### 2. Adam at Event (if possible)
- Take Adam to a game/practice
- Film crowd interactions
- Get fan reactions

#### 3. Adam Dances
- Pick trending audio
- Film clean dance video
- Multiple angles

#### 4. Adam Greets
- Film wave/greeting sequence
- Get someone to walk toward Adam
- Capture the interaction

#### 5. Adam High-Fives
- Line up people for high-fives
- Film the reactions
- Get behind-the-scenes too

### Filming Tips
- Shoot vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels
- Get close-ups of Adam's movements
- Capture people's genuine reactions
- Film more than you need

## Day 4-5: Analyze & Iterate

### Track These Metrics
- Views
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Watch time / completion rate
- Follower growth

### Questions to Answer
1. Which reaction gets the best response?
2. What content format works?
3. What time of day is best?
4. What audio/trends should we try?

### Iterate
Based on what works:
- Double down on winners
- Try variations
- Add new reactions if needed
